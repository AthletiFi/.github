# Pleyr Community Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Pledge

We at **AthletiFi Sport Inc.**, the creators of **Pleyr**, are committed to fostering an inclusive, welcoming, and respectful community for everyone. We pledge to make participation in our projects and community a harassment-free experience, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all who participate in our community, as well as the consequences for unacceptable behavior. By participating, you agree to uphold this code.

## 2. Expected Behavior

- **Be Respectful**: Treat everyone with respect, kindness, and consideration in all interactions.
- **Be Collaborative**: Work constructively with fellow community members. Disagreements are normal, but a positive and collaborative spirit is essential.
- **Be Open-Minded**: Be receptive to constructive feedback and differing viewpoints.
- **Be Responsible**: Take responsibility for your words, your actions, and their consequences.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behaviors are subject to enforcement and include, but are not limited to:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion.
- Inappropriate use of sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Threats or incitement of violence.
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, talks, or other events.
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or community setting.

## 4. Reporting Violations

If you are subjected to or witness any violations of this Code of Conduct, or have any other concerns, please contact us immediately at **<conduct@pleyr.com>**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially. Our team will take whatever action is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances, and we are committed to protecting reporters from retaliation.

## 5. Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior from any community member will not be tolerated. Anyone asked to stop such behavior is expected to comply immediately.

If a community member engages in unacceptable behavior, the project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including a temporary or permanent ban from the community and its platforms without warning.

## 6. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, both online and offline, including but not limited to GitHub repositories, social media platforms, and any events where community members are representing Pleyr.

## 7. Contact Information

For any queries related to this Code of Conduct, please contact us at **<info@pleyr.com>**.
